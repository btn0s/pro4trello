Why should you support development?

Pro for Trello is a free Google Chrome extension. It will always be free. You will likely appreciate that:

  • Your Trello experience remains ads-free
  • There are no banners obstructing your work
  • You are not forced to make any sacrifices or compromises as all settings are customizable - so you chose what you use.
  • The extension is safe, code is OPEN and it is not trying to steal or sell your data or inject affiliate cookies or track you
  • The author is maintaining the extension and is as responsive as he can achieve considering he is running multple projects and a business

Whether or not you decide to support the author will not likely change the above. But it sure could make a difference.

How can you support development?

If you're supercharging Trello on a daily basis with Pro for Trello you should consider makinga symbolic pledge via Patreon since it's making your life easier - and become a supporter. If you're using it for work or even making money with the help of Pro for Trello features you could consider a bigger pledge - and become a dedicated or a hardcdore supporter.

How does the author use funds?

The funds from Patreon pledges will be used to purchase licences for software dewvelopment tools (these are now all monthly/yearly, one-time purchases are a thing of the past), pay for website hosting, online courses and tutorials, as well as purchasing licences for using content (e.g. images, videos, music).

Have in mind that by supporting the author you are not only supporting the development of Pro4Trello but also development and maintenance of other FREE tools and games. You can learn more at https://apptorium.net.
